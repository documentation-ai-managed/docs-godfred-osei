## Overview

Organize your documentation with categories and pages.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create categories">
    ````
    Click "Add Category" in your project sidebar.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add pages">
    ````
    Click "New Page" and assign it to a category.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reorder items">
    ````
    Drag and drop categories/pages to reorder as needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
