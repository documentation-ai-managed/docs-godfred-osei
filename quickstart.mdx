## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Git CLI installed for advanced file uploads

</Callout>

## Create Your Account and First Space

Follow these steps to register, log in, and set up your documentation space. The entire process takes under 5 minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">

    Visit [https://app.godfredosei.com](https://app.godfredosei.com) and click **Sign Up**.

    Fill in your details:

    | Field       | Example                  |
    |-------------|--------------------------|
    | Email       | user@example.com        |
    | Password    | StrongPassword123!      |
    | Full Name   | Your Name               |

    Verify your email and proceed to login.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">

    Enter your credentials on the login page.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable two-factor authentication for added security after your first login.
    </Callout>

  </Step>

  <Step title="Create Documentation Space" icon="plus">

    From the dashboard, click **New Space**.

    Enter:
    - Space Name: `My Project Docs`
    - Description: `Documentation for my project`

    Select **Public** or **Private** visibility, then create the space.

  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Initial Files" icon="upload">

    In your new space, use the file uploader.

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Drag & Drop" icon="mouse-pointer">
        Drag your `README.mdx` and other MDX files into the upload area.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="CLI Upload" icon="terminal">
        Use the Godfred Osei CLI:

````bash
npm install -g @godfredosei/cli
godfred login --email user@example.com
godfred space upload MyProjectDocs ./docs/
````

      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

  </Step>
</Steps>
